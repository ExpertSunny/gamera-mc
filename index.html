How to Join

1

Open Minecraft

Launch the Java Edition of Minecraft on your computer

2

Go to Multiplayer

Click on the "Multiplayer" button from the main menu

3

Add Server

Click "Add Server" and enter our server information

4

Enter Server Address

Type mc.gamera.co.in as the server address

5

Join the Fun!

Click "Done" and double-click on our server to join

Server Rules

Be Respectful

Treat other players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or excessive profanity.

No Griefing

Destroying or altering other players' builds without permission is strictly prohibited.

No Cheating

Usage of hacks, mods that give unfair advantages, or exploits is not allowed.

No Advertising

Promoting other servers or unsolicited advertising is not permitted.

No Going to the End

The End dimension is off-limits to maintain server balance and preserve dragon events.

Report Issues

Report rule violations or technical issues to staff on our Discord server.

Violation of these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server depending on severity and frequency. Our staff has the final say on all moderation decisions.
